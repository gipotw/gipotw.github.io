About Us

Grand Intellectual Property Office (GIPO) started-up at Taipei City, and has worked in the field of patent, trademark prosecution and other legal cases for more than ten years. As a rapidly grown company, our goal is to provide our clients with the most professional and the highest quality service, as well as minimized costs. We work only on projects which can be conducted by us with high level of competence expected by clients, and obtain many excellent praises and evaluations.

GIPO has professional teams in the fields of patent, trademark, and law. We provide a wide range of patent/trademark services, focusing on patent/trademark prosecution in Taiwan, China, Japan, U.S.A., etc for our domestic and overseas clients. Our practice is broad-ranging, from all aspects of patent, trademark, and litigation, to advice on patent law, domain names, licensing, and related aspects of intellectual property law. We collaborate with our clients in different ways, such as innovative thinking process, increasing margins of profit via patent/trademark right, and so on, to meet their needs.

Our focused approach to preparation and prosecution of patent/trademark applications helps clients successfully minimize the risk of patent/trademark infringement while bringing new products to market. At the same time, our astute patent/trademark portfolio analyses and strategic patent/trademark counseling help clients develop and maintain patent/trademark portfolios and positions that generate increased margins and growth. Our comprehensive knowledge of global intellectual property law, our moderate size that facilitates personalized service and close attorney/agent-client relationships, and our dedication to client service has kept us in the forefront of our profession for many years.

In addition, GIPO has a lot of patent attorneys/patent agents from different technical backgrounds to deal with various cases from any fields, such as semiconductor industry, artificial intelligence, machinery industry, automatic control, communication device, medical appliance, digital image, solar/fuel cell, chemistry, material engineering, and biotechnology etc. Also, we provide strategic advices with our clients on reducing their patent rights into practice and help them develop and manage their patent/trademark portfolios both in Taiwan and internationally.

Our price policy is transparent and flexible to meet needs and budgets of clients. While providing our principals with terms and conditions of our work we are guided by their priorities: expected quality of services, experience of GIPO, experience in clients’ business industry, expected standards of performance and cost efficiency. We are able to deliver work results promptly, running to agreed budgets and keeping time-limits set.

With highly specialized knowledge and in-depth understanding of clients’ businesses, we support the technological innovations that are driving the world economy. With GIPO there are no surprises. Our clients – whether Fortune 100 companies or select high tech start-ups – benefit from our commitment to adhering to agreed-upon costs, scheduling, reporting and other engagement-specific provisions. We know how to aggressively protect the assets that give clients’ business its competitive edge—with extensive experience and intimate knowledge of domestic and international judicial systems. Whatever our role, our goal is simple: to support and protect those whose innovations propel the world forward!
