Advantage

I. MORE THAN 10 YEARS OF SUCCESS

GIPO has an excellent team including patent attorneys/patent agents, and is the best in the field of patent specification/claim drafting, trademark prosecution/litigation and other IP-related areas.

II. EXCLUSIVE SERVICE AND REASONABLE SERVICE FEE

Patent attorneys/patent agents in GIPO always discuss technical features with clients and conduct specification/claim drafting themselves, and offer the best services and reasonable service fee to you.

III. PROFESSIONAL TEAMS

Since GIPO handles every case professionally and employs open management, many in-house patent and R&D engineers are pleased to collaborate with our patent attorneys/patent agents.

Intellectual property knows no borders. Therefore, GIPO collaborates globally with a network of foreign experts and colleagues. Thus, we support you in more than 100 countries in applying for and enforcing your intellectual property rights.

You can rely on the quality of our work. This is because GIPO has been certified in accordance with ISO 9001 since 2008, which is reviewed and confirmed annually. This quality management system is a well-proven instrument. This especially applies with regard to customer orientation, leadership responsibility, and continuous improvement. In addition, it improves both the transparency of our internal processes and the satisfaction of our clients.
